Photon Phanatics is a puzzle game for iOS requiring you to direct light to solar panels using a variety of different fixtures. The base game is free and contains 50 levels of varying difficulties to let you try it out. Once you complete them all, you can make a one time purchase of $1.99 to unlock all current and future levels. Get it on the app store

An easy to use level editor is included with the game allowing you to create your own puzzles and share them with friends.
