Equip your character with collectable parts to battle with friends on the couch or online in this 2d, twin stick, platformer shooting game. More details, screenshots, and videos coming soon.
Features
  • Collect, craft, and earn hundreds of parts
  • Outfit your character with parts from your collection
  • Fight with a variety of weapons
  • Tight twin stick, platforming controls
  • Fast paced 2d shooting action
  • Up to 16 player online matches
  • 4 player split screen local or online
  • Wide selection of maps, themes, and game types
  • Fully customizable matches and attributes
  • 4k and 60 fps graphics
  • Prerelease coming soon to PC and XBox One
